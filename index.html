Who We Are

From 3D foundations to perception, reasoning, and action

EngineeringAI Lab believes that truly intelligent systems must understand, model, and interact with the three-dimensional physical world. We study high-quality 3D scene foundations, 3D representation and manipulation, agentic spatial understanding and synthesis, interactive dynamics and motion, and LLM-driven decision intelligence.

Our work connects frontier research with practical needs in robotics simulation, embodied intelligence, games, film, and virtual content production. The goal is to move AI from foundational construction to spatial understanding, then toward autonomous decision-making and action in complex tasks.

Mission

Research Directions

01

3D Foundation

We develop general-purpose 3D foundation models for cross-modal generation and understanding, from 2D observations to structured 3D assets.

  • Image-to-3D: reconstruct high-quality 3D assets from one or more images
  • Part-aware 3D: enable semantic decomposition, editing, and recomposition
02

Agentic Scene Understanding

We explore how AI agents understand intent, call tools, and generate complex 3D scenes while building evaluation and simulation-data systems for spatial cognition.

  • Agent-based automatic 3D scene construction
  • Engine-based scene understanding tools and benchmarks
03

Interactive Dynamics and Motion

We model realistic dynamic interactions for digital humans and multi-character environments, supporting animation, game NPCs, and embodied intelligence scenarios.

  • 2D/3D Talking Head: natural expression and lip-motion generation
  • Human-human interaction: plausible multi-person motion sequences
04

LLM Decision Intelligence

We connect LLM reasoning with reinforcement learning, 3D understanding, and task planning so intelligent systems can reason, decide, and act in complex environments.

  • LLM reasoning for scene-level task planning
  • RL-driven 3D understanding and interaction
