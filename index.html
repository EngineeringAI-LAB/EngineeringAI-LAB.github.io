About

A focused lab for 3D world model research

EngineeringAI Lab explores open 3D world models with generative AI and embodied intelligence. We believe that intelligent systems should learn not only from text and abstract reasoning, but also from vision, action, spatial feedback, and interaction with the world.

The lab is led by Huadong Mo and Zhenhong Sun. Zhenhong Sun serves as the main research lead for the shared 3D World Model agenda, aligning the team's work across 3D representation, spatial synthesis, motion, and decision intelligence.

Projects

Four directions and representative systems

Our projects follow the same four directions as Zhenhong Sun's LingJing 3D World Model agenda, turning a research vision into coordinated lab systems.

01

Foundation Representation and Manipulation

Compact and controllable 3D asset representations for reconstruction, editing, decomposition, and state prediction.

  • PyramidVoxel and PyramidVAE + DiT
  • Part-aware 3D structure decomposition
  • Topology-aware asset manipulation
02

Spatial Understanding and Synthesis

Agentic generation pipelines and engine-grounded tools for coherent 3D scenes, simulation data, and spatial evaluation.

  • StoryBlender for editable 3D story worlds
  • Camera and viewpoint modeling
  • Scene understanding tools and benchmarks
03

Interaction Dynamics and Motion

Expressive avatars, human-scene interaction, and multi-person motion generation for dynamic digital worlds.

  • XTalker and 3DXTalker
  • Human-human interaction generation
  • Viseme metrics and avatar evaluation
04

Decision Intelligence and Reasoning

LLM-based reasoning, language-guided planning, reinforcement learning, and decision systems for agents in 3D worlds.

  • LLM-based world reasoning
  • Language-supervised decision learning
  • RL-driven 3D interaction

Publications

Selected team papers and manuscripts

StoryBlender is our first team-led EngineeringAI Lab paper accepted to ECCV, and it anchors the lab's broader publication pipeline around 3D world models.

ECCV | Team Milestone

StoryBlender: Inter-Shot Consistent and Editable 3D Storyboard with Spatial-temporal Dynamics

EngineeringAI Lab's first team-led paper accepted to ECCV. The work studies narrative-to-3D scene construction, editable multi-shot storyboards, and spatial-temporal consistency.

Preprint

3DXTalker: Unifying Identity, Lip Sync, Emotion, and Spatial Dynamics in Expressive 3D Talking Avatars

A 3D talking-avatar system connecting identity, lip motion, emotion, head movement, and spatial dynamics.

Manuscript

XTalker: Turn, Smile, and Speak in Controllable Talking Portrait Animation

A controllable talking-portrait animation framework for expressive and synchronized face generation.

Manuscript

P2Voxel and topology-aware 3D part decomposition

Ongoing work on compact 3D mesh tokenization, 3D foundation representations, and structure-aware asset decomposition.
