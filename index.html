01 | Who We Are

A focused team for 3D world model research and engineering

02 | We Work On

Four shared directions for open 3D world models

Our research agenda follows the same four directions as Zhenhong Sun's LingJing 3D World Model work, turning personal research vision into a coordinated lab program.

01

Foundation Representation and Manipulation

We study compact, controllable representations of 3D assets as the foundation for reconstruction, editing, decomposition, and state prediction in 3D world models.

  • Unified 3D asset representations
  • Single-view and multi-view reconstruction
  • Part-aware structure decomposition
02

Spatial Understanding and Synthesis

We build coherent 3D worlds through agentic generation pipelines and engine-grounded tools that help agents perceive static scene structures and dynamic world events.

  • Compositional 3D scene generation
  • Engine-grounded spatial perception
  • Simulation data and evaluation
03

Interaction Dynamics and Motion

We model expressive avatars, human-scene interaction, and multi-person behavioral dynamics so digital worlds can move, respond, and evolve.

  • 2D and 3D talking avatars
  • Human-scene interaction modeling
  • Multi-agent motion and social dynamics
04

Decision Intelligence and Reasoning

We study LLM-based reasoning, language-guided planning, reinforcement learning, and decision-making systems for agents acting in 3D worlds.

  • LLM-based world reasoning
  • Language-guided planning
  • Decision making in 3D environments
